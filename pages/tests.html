Preparation

Test Preparation Guidelines

Ensure accurate diagnostic results by following these simple preparatory instructions.

Blood & Pathology

  • Fasting required for 10-12 hours for Lipid & Glucose tests.
  • Only plain water is allowed during fasting periods.
  • Avoid alcohol and high-fat meals 24 hours prior.

Radiology & Scans

  • Remove all metallic objects, jewelry, and watches.
  • Wear loose, comfortable clothing (gowns provided).
  • Inform technicians of any metallic implants or pacemakers.

Cardiology (ECG/Echo)

  • Avoid applying skin creams, lotions, or oils on chest.
  • Wear easily removable shirts or upper garments.
  • Relax completely and sit quietly before the test.

Urine & Hormones

  • First-morning mid-stream urine sample is highly preferred.
  • Use the sterile collection container provided by the lab.
  • Note specific times if serial hormonal samples are required.
