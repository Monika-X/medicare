Sample Integrity

Temperature-Controlled Cold-Chain Technology

Your biological samples are treated with laboratory-grade precision from the moment of collection to delivery at our main lab.

2°C to 8°C Maintained

Certified insulated transit boxes maintain a precise temperature range to prevent any degradation of proteins, enzymes, and biochemical markers in your blood samples.

Double-Barcode Tracking

Every vial receives two unique barcodes linking directly to your Patient ID, ensuring absolute traceability throughout the lab processing chain and eliminating mix-up risks entirely.

Bio-Hazard Safe Disposal

Used needles, lancets, and collection consumables are immediately sealed in puncture-proof bio-hazard containers on-site and handled by licensed medical waste disposal operators.

90-Minute Lab Transit

Our strategically located mobile collection fleet ensures your samples reach the central lab within 90 minutes of draw time, maintaining peak sample viability for accurate analysis.

Clinical Safety

Phlebotomist Safety & Hygiene Standards

Every home visit follows our rigorous 12-point clinical safety checklist — non-negotiable for every appointment.

Single-Use Sterile Needles

Needles are sealed until opened in front of you and immediately disposed in bio-hazard containers.

Pre-Visit Hand Sanitization

Phlebotomists sanitize hands before and after glove application at your location.

Fresh PPE Per Visit

Fresh gloves and N95 masks are used for every individual patient visit, no exceptions.

Vaccinated Staff Only

All field phlebotomists are fully vaccinated and undergo monthly health and fitness screening.

Bedside Double-Barcoding

Common Queries

Home Collection FAQs

Specific answers to the most common questions our patients ask about doorstep diagnostics.

What time should I be ready for a morning collection?

Our phlebotomist will arrive within a 30-minute window of your booked slot. For 6:00-8:00 AM morning slots (most popular for fasting tests), we recommend having your ID and requisition ready the evening before. You will receive an SMS confirmation 30 minutes before the technician arrives at your door.

Can I book home collection for multiple family members?

Yes. Our family booking option allows up to 4 patients to be registered under a single home visit appointment. Each family member gets their own unique Patient ID and barcode. This is particularly cost-effective for household health checkups and senior citizen care, and attracts discounted collection fees per additional member.

What should I prepare at home before the visit?

Please ensure you have your government-issued photo ID ready, maintain the required fasting period if applicable, and wear a loose-fitting top with sleeves that can be easily rolled up above the elbow. Have a clean chair or table ready for the technician and ensure the area is well-lit. No other preparation is required — our phlebotomist brings everything else.

Is home collection available for radiology tests?

Home sample collection is available for all blood-based pathology, urine, and stool tests. Radiology and imaging tests such as MRI, CT scan, X-ray, Ultrasound, and ECG require specialized equipment and must be performed at our diagnostic center. You may call us to schedule a preferred time slot for center visits with minimum waiting.
