Prep Guide

Appointment Preparation Checklist

Ensure a hassle-free diagnostic sample extraction by double-checking these instructions.

Fasting Checklist

If your selected test is a lipid profile or glucose blood panel, maintain a strict fast of 10 to 12 hours. Do not consume tea, coffee, juice, or food. Only plain water is permitted.

Identification & Prescription

Keep a valid photo ID card (Aadhar, PAN, or Passport) ready. If the diagnostic test has been prescribed by a physician, please keep the physical prescription paper handy.

Attire Guidelines

Wear loose, comfortable clothing with short sleeves or sleeves that can be easily rolled up past the elbow for comfortable venipuncture.

Clinical Safety

door-step collection Safety Standards

We prioritize your safety above everything. Our clinical home visit phlebotomists follow rigorous medical directives to ensure a sterile, pain-free sample draw at your convenience.

100% Sterile, Sealed Kits

Each collection needle is opened directly in front of the patient and discarded in bio-hazard bins immediately.

Double Barcoding System

Vials are barcoded at your bedside with your unique Patient ID, completely preventing manual mix-ups.

Temperature-Controlled Transit

All samples are immediate sealed in certified cold-chain transit cases keeping a steady temperature between 2°C to 8°C. This prevents degradation during transport, ensuring absolute lab accuracy.
