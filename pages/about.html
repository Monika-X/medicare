Our Story

A Legacy of Trust and Precision

Founded in 1995, MediCare Diagnostic Center began with a simple mission: to provide the community with the most accurate, reliable, and swift medical diagnostic services available.

Over the decades, we have grown from a single regional laboratory into a comprehensive healthcare network. We continually invest in cutting-edge technology and highly skilled professionals to ensure that your health is in the safest hands.
