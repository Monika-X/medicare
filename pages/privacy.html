1. Introduction

Welcome to MediCare Diagnostic Center ("we," "our," or "us"). We are deeply committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This privacy notice explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data when you visit our website, book our services, or interact with our diagnostic facilities.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please navigate to the Contact DPO section to reach our Data Protection Officer.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website, express an interest in obtaining information about us, or when you contact us. The personal information we collect falls into the following categories:

Identity Data

First name, last name, username, title, date of birth, age, gender, and government-issued ID numbers (Aadhar/PAN).

Contact Data

Billing address, home collection address, email address, and telephone/mobile numbers.

Health Data

Past medical history, current symptoms, prescriptions, and previous diagnostic test results if uploaded by you.

Financial Data

Payment card details and UPI IDs. (Note: These are processed securely by our PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways; we do not store full card numbers).

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information for a variety of business and medical purposes, relying on our legitimate business interests, medical necessity, and/or for compliance with our legal obligations:

  • To facilitate test bookings: We use your contact and location data to schedule center visits and dispatch phlebotomists for home collection.
  • To perform diagnostic analysis: Health and identity data are necessary to accurately process samples, interpret results, and generate correct clinical reports.
  • To deliver digital reports: We use your email and mobile number to send secure links, OTPs, and PDF downloads of your test results.
  • To process payments: To manage billing, issue GST invoices, and process insurance cashless claims.
  • To send administrative information: Such as appointment reminders, changes to our terms, conditions, and policies.

4. Data Security (HIPAA)

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal and medical information we process. Our systems are fully compliant with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) standards.

End-to-End Encryption

All patient data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 standard. Data in transit is protected via TLS 1.3 protocols.

Strict Access Control

Only authorized laboratory personnel and your assigned doctor (if applicable) can access your clinical test reports via multi-factor authentication.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you possess specific rights regarding your personal health information:

Right to Access

You have the right to request copies of your medical records and personal data held by us at any time via your patient portal.

Right to Rectification

You can request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.

Right to Erasure

You may request the deletion of your account and personal data, subject to mandatory medical record retention laws (typically 5 years for pathology reports).

6. Contact Data Protection Officer

If you have questions or comments about this notice, wish to exercise any of your privacy rights, or want to report a data security concern, you may contact our dedicated Data Protection Officer (DPO).
