General Questions

Booking & Scheduling

Answers to your most common questions about appointments and procedures.

You can book a test through our website by clicking the "Book Test" button, or by calling our toll-free support line at 1800-123-456. Walk-ins are also accepted at all MediCare centers. For home collection appointments, online booking through our website is highly recommended for a faster confirmation.
Most routine and preventive health checkups do not require a prescription. However, certain specialized tests such as prenatal markers (Double Marker, Triple Marker), targeted biopsy, and specialized hormone assays may require a valid physician's prescription. Our booking team will confirm any such requirements when you call or book online.
While walk-ins are welcome for most routine blood tests, we strongly recommend booking an appointment in advance to minimize your waiting time and ensure sample collection equipment is pre-prepared for your specific test. For radiology scans like MRI, CT, and 2D Echo, appointments are mandatory.
Fasting requirements depend on the specific test. Tests like Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS), Lipid Profile, and Kidney Function require 10-12 hours of fasting (plain water is allowed). Tests such as CBC, Thyroid Profile, and HbA1c generally do not require fasting. Our online booking confirmation will clearly state the preparation guidelines for your selected test.
Yes. You can reschedule your booked appointment free of charge up to 2 hours before your scheduled time slot via your patient dashboard. For cancellations made before the phlebotomist departs for home collection, a 100% refund is processed back to your original payment method within 2-3 business days.
Please carry a valid government-issued photo ID such as Aadhar Card, PAN Card, or Passport. If the test has been prescribed by a physician, please bring the original physical prescription as well. Your booking confirmation SMS or email will also serve as a reference document at the center.
Billing & Payments

Insurance, Cashless & Payments

Everything you need to know about payment methods and insurance coverage.

We accept cashless claims from major insurance providers including Star Health, HDFC Ergo, ICICI Lombard, United India, New India Assurance, Niva Bupa, Bajaj Allianz, and Tata AIG. Please inform our billing team about your insurance provider at the time of booking so they can verify your policy coverage in advance.
We accept all major payment modes including cash, debit and credit cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and NEFT/RTGS transfers for corporate accounts. Online bookings via our website support all major card networks. Receipts are issued digitally via email for all transactions.
Yes. Our pre-configured health packages are already offered at 30-40% discounts compared to booking individual tests. Additional seasonal discounts and family bundle deals are announced periodically. Senior citizens above 60 years receive a standard 10% discount on all services. Corporate group bookings receive custom pricing upon inquiry.
Refunds for cancelled appointments are processed automatically. If payment was made via card or UPI, the refund is credited within 2-3 business days. Cash payments are refunded as a credit to your patient wallet on our portal, which can be used for future bookings, or transferred directly to your bank account upon request via our billing helpline.
All prices displayed on our website are exclusive of applicable GST. A 5% service tax is calculated and displayed transparently in your booking summary before final confirmation. Your digital receipt will provide a fully itemized breakup of the test fee, any applicable collection fee, and the tax component for complete clarity.
Digital Reports

Accessing & Understanding Reports

Everything about downloading, sharing, and interpreting your diagnostic results.

Most routine blood test reports are available within 12-24 hours from the time of sample collection. Specialized tests such as cultures, allergy profiles, and prenatal markers may take 48-72 hours. Radiology reports (MRI, CT, USG) are typically ready within 24-48 hours. You will receive an SMS and email notification with a secure download link once your report is certified and uploaded.
Log in to our patient portal using your registered mobile number or email address. Navigate to the "My Reports" section in your dashboard to view, download, and print any of your historical reports in PDF format. Reports are stored securely for up to 5 years in your account for easy future reference.
Yes. From your dashboard, you can generate a secure shareable link or directly email the PDF report to your physician from our portal. If your doctor is part of our registered medical partner network, they can access your reports directly through our Doctor Portal using your unique Patient ID. This eliminates any need to physically carry printed reports.
If you need a report urgently, please inform our lab team at the time of sample collection. For most critical parameters, a preliminary verbal report is communicated directly to the patient or attending physician if abnormal values are detected. You may also call our report helpline at reports@medicare.com or +91-1800-123-456 to request priority processing for time-sensitive cases.
Yes, all our digitally issued reports are legally valid. They carry the certified digital signature of our Senior Pathologist or Radiologist, unique report ID, and QR verification code. These reports are accepted by hospitals, insurance providers, courts, and government bodies as official diagnostic documentation under NABL and ISO certification standards.
Doorstep Services

Home Sample Collection

Detailed answers about our phlebotomy safety, timing, and service coverage.

Absolutely. Our phlebotomists are fully vaccinated, undergo monthly health screening, and follow strict clinical hygiene protocols. They wear fresh gloves and masks for every patient, use only sealed single-use sterile needles opened directly in front of you, and immediately dispose of sharps in certified bio-hazard containers. All kits are sealed and tamper-evident until the moment of use.
Home collection services are available from 6:00 AM to 11:00 AM, which is the optimal window for fasting-based tests. If your selected tests do not require fasting, extended slots up to 5:00 PM may be available on request. Early morning slots (6:00-8:00 AM) are the most popular and should be booked at least 24 hours in advance to guarantee availability.
Our home collection teams operate within a 20-kilometer radius of all our main diagnostic centers. Collection is available for over 120+ active pin codes across the city. Use the pin code checker on our Sample Collection page to instantly confirm whether your area is covered. For locations beyond the active zone, a nominal transport surcharge may apply upon special request.
After collection, samples are double-barcoded with your unique Patient ID and immediately sealed in certified temperature-controlled cold-chain transit cases. These insulated sample boxes maintain a steady temperature between 2 degrees Celsius and 8 degrees Celsius throughout the journey to the lab. This prevents any biochemical degradation and guarantees the absolute integrity of your test results.
A standard home collection convenience fee of Rs. 1,450 applies per visit. However, home collection is provided free of charge for bookings with a total test value above Rs. 4,835 within a 15-kilometer radius. For corporate accounts or family group bookings, complimentary home collection is included as part of the wellness partnership agreement regardless of the booking total.
