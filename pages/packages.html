Help Center

Packages Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions regarding comprehensive health checkups.

Is fasting required for all comprehensive packages?

Yes, fasting for 10 to 12 hours is mandatory. These packages include fasting blood sugar and lipid profiles which require a baseline state to deliver clinically accurate evaluations.

Can I swap a test in the package for another one?

Standard health packages are pre-configured to offer the highest clinical value at optimal rates, so substitutions are not supported. However, you can add individual tests to your package during booking.

How soon will I receive my package results?

A complete digital report will be uploaded to your patient dashboard within 12-24 hours. You will receive an SMS and email notification with a direct download link once the diagnostic analysis is certified.

Need a Customized Health Panel?

Our medical consulting staff can help you package custom blood profiles and radiology scans to support specific therapeutic checks or personal preventative tracking.
